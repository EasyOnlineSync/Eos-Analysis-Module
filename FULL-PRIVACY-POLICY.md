# Privacy Policy – Eos Analysis Module

Eos Analysis Module does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.
The only information transmitted is the video URL and title, sent to the native host to perform the local download.
This data is not retained, not shared with third parties, and is only processed upon explicit user action (clicking the “Download” button).
No browsing history, account information, or personal data is collected by the extension.
